Text Case Converter

Easily convert your text between different cases: uppercase, lowercase, title case, sentence case, and toggle case.

About This Tool

This text case converter allows you to:

  • UPPERCASE: Convert all text to capital letters
  • lowercase: Convert all text to small letters
  • Title Case: Capitalize the first letter of each word
  • Sentence case: Capitalize the first letter of each sentence
  • Toggle Case: Switch between uppercase and lowercase for each character
